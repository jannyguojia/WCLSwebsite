Year-end Awards Ceremony Notice

10-12-2023

Dear teachers, parents and dear students: Things to note at the year-end awards conference this Sunday (December 10) are as follows: (1) There will be no classroom classes on the day of the awards. Please go directly to the award ceremony.

(2) Admission starts at 2 o'clock in the afternoon. The seating areas have been divided according to classes. Students are asked to sit according to the arrangements of the on-site teachers. (3) The award ceremony includes the awarding of awards for each grade and carefully prepared programs for each class. During the award ceremony, please try not to move in and out unless necessary, and keep the venue quiet.

(4) Award Ceremony Venue: Maclaurin LT 103 (please see the attached picture). (5) Please note: Due to security reasons on weekends, most gates in Victoria cannot be opened. Please refer to the route marked in our picture (please pay attention to the two entrance locations marked in the picture, the nearest entrance is Alan MacDiarmid); suggestions After everyone arrives at the school, continue driving up Kelburn Parade. After passing the roundabout, you can drive into the Victoria University parking lot from the entrance on the left. Teachers and parent volunteers will be on hand to provide tips and guidance and seek help if needed.
