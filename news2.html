"Chinese Week" series of activities - "Yue" reading session of picture book stories

September 17th to 23rd is New Zealand Chinese Week. In order to cooperate with the school's Chinese Week activities, the book4 class conducted a "joy" reading session of picture book stories under the leadership of teacher Guo Jia. The content includes activities such as picture book story reading, picture book story translation, and picture book story creation.

The little "translator" Zhou Yinuo told everyone a short English story in Chinese vividly. The accurate language expression showed the solid Chinese skills of the young students.
