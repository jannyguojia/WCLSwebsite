WCLS Classes

"The Wellington Chinese Language School (WCLS) is a community-basedschool teaching both oral and written Mandarin Chinese. Since itsestablishment in 1972, the school has grown to 29 classes and 505students (2019). We offer classes at all levels from beginner toadvanced, from the age of four to adults. Classes are currently heldon Sundays at Victoria University of wellington, Kelburn Parade. Allclasses are taught by qualified teachers who are native Mandarinspeakers.

"We welcome students of Chinese origin and others who are interestedin increasing their knowledge of Chinese language and culture. weprovide many valuable opportunities to practice the Chinese languageand experience Chinese culture,including dance teams, a band,andspecial interest classes, such as Chinese calligraphy, painting,chess and paper cutting. Parents can practice Tai Chi (tutored by anexperienced instructor) while their child is learning Chinese at thelanguage school. Enrolments are also accepted throughout the yeardepending on place availability.
